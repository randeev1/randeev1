What is JoJo's Bizarre Adventure?

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a hit anime series based on the manga by Hirohiko Araki. It spans across six generations of "JoJos" starting from Jonathan Joestar to the newly announced Jolyne Cujoh for the anime. The manga is now on its eigth generation which is Josuke "Gappy" Higashikata.

The Current JoJo's Are:

  1. Jonathan Joestar
  2. Joseph Joestar
  3. Jotaro Kujo
  4. Josuke Higashikata
  5. Giorno Giovanna
  6. Jolyne Cujo
